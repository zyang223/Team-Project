Excel DCF Template

Download the Excel DCF template to get started:

The following model_template in Excel has three different models:

  • The first model is a typical DCF model with switches on street-base, conservative case, and optimistic case. For using the model, you need to make your own hypothesis or find out the relative financial data from Bloomberg or Capital IQ or the company's financial statement.


  • The second model is a relative evaluation for the comparable companies. It uses multiples and ratios of trailing and leading to account for a company's value. You have to be very careful in selecting the peer companies (using the screen function) or you could also adjust the percentage on variance to get a rational result.


  • The third model is a benchmark for the relative evaluation. It provides you with insight and visualization of where your target companies stand among the peer and comparable companies.
